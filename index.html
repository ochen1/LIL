Leaders
In
Literacy

Leaders in Literacy is a youth organization that encourages leadership, literacy and engagement in young people. Currently based in Edmonton, Alberta, we offer free educational programs and resources to youth across the province.

Writing Competitions

This summer, we will be hosting the first of our annual writing competitions. It is open to writers across Alberta who are looking for an opportunity to showcase their talent and possibly win prizes. Following an overarching theme, there will be three categories for two different age groups. For more information, check out our competitions page.

Reading
Classes

Throughout the year, we hold weekly reading classes which are hosted by Emily Yang and Clara Chen. It is open to all students from grades 6-9. At these weekly sessions, students read and analyze short stories, poems, and short films.
