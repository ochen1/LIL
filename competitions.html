Greetings!

Welcome to the Leaders in Literacy Summer Writing Competition 2022, brought to you by Leaders in Literacy! Whether you're a seasoned writer, or truly enjoy expressing your thoughts through pen and paper, this competition is catered to you. We offer multiple writing categories for the competition, as well as separate divisions, assuring that your piece is as best as it can be. Lastly, in order to ensure that you are prepared for the competition, our team will be hosting one Writing Workshop per Category, all held by Alice Yang.
